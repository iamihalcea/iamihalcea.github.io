About

I'm a software engineer and I started this blog as a means of motivating myself to dig deeper into topics that I enjoy or find interesting. Most of my interests revolve around security in the context of software engineering - everything from hardware security solutions like TPMs, to SQL injection attacks. This is not to say that I expect or want anyone else would find any of the stuff I write useful.

Apart from technical topics, I write the occasional post^Wrant on economics, psychology or anything else that sparks my fancy. These are obviously 100% expert opinions, served to the internet straight from my armchair as I sip a measure of bourbon.

If you do think that I've missed the point in an article and would like to share that knowledge, feel free to contact me here.

Note: All of the thoughts expressed on this blog are my own. Neither my employer nor any other institutions that I'm affiliated to have any involvement in this.
